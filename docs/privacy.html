Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 15, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to the AxelBase URL Encoder – Decoder, a fully client-side web utility designed to safely encode and decode text using RFC 3986 percent-encoding. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the tool. The core principle of the utility is simple: your data never leaves your device. The application runs entirely in your browser without servers, databases, logs, or analytics of any kind.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

The URL Encoder – Decoder operates under a strict zero-data-collection policy. We do not collect, store, or access:

  • Personal Identifiable Information (PII): Names, emails, phone numbers, IP addresses, or physical addresses.
  • Technical Data: Browser fingerprints, device information, session data, or geographic data.
  • Content Data: Any text, URLs, encoded strings, or decoded outputs you enter into the tool.
  • Analytics: No Google Analytics, no tracking pixels, no logging of user behavior.

All operations occur strictly client-side, ensuring full privacy and data isolation.

3. How the Tool Works

The tool performs all encoding and decoding locally using JavaScript functions such as encodeURIComponent combined with strict RFC 3986 rules. Auto-detection, Swap Mode, and shareable link features also operate inside your browser without remote calls.

  • Processing: Your text is processed instantly within your session.
  • No Transmission: Nothing is uploaded to our servers or any external service.
  • Share Links: When creating a shareable link, content is Base64-encoded directly in the URL. We never receive or store it.
  • Clipboard: Copy actions use browser APIs and never send data away from your device.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The URL Encoder – Decoder does not use cookies, localStorage, fingerprinting, ads, or telemetry systems. Your session is completely stateless, and nothing persists after closing or refreshing the page.

5. Data Security

Because no data is collected or transmitted, risk is minimized. However, we encourage users to:

  • Use trusted and updated browsers.
  • Avoid pasting extremely sensitive content on shared devices.
  • Clear the page or close the tab after handling confidential data.

6. Children's Privacy

The tool is not directed toward children under 13. Since no information is collected, we cannot knowingly store or process children’s data.

7. International Users

Because the tool collects no data, it naturally complies with international privacy regulations such as GDPR, CCPA, and others by default.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect improvements or legal requirements. Updates will be posted with a new effective date.

By using the URL Encoder – Decoder, you acknowledge and agree to this no-data-collection policy.
